Yongjian Zhang received the B.Eng. degree from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) in 2021. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering at SYSU. His current research focuses on 3D reconstruction, including stereo matching, depth estimation, and dense matching.
